Info

Random target touch test for ataxia and upper-limb control. Targets appear at random positions; the system logs time-to-target, time in target, final error, tremor, and smoothness. Runs are scored using a SARA-like composite (0 = no impairment, 4 = severe) that combines final error, reaction time, percent time inside the target, tremor, and movement smoothness.
